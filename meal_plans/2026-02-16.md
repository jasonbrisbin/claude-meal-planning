## Meal Prep Tips
- **Sunday prep**: Hard boil 1 dozen eggs, cook white rice in bulk, wash and chop vegetables
- **Mid-week prep** (Wednesday): Prepare turkey chili, hard boil second dozen eggs
- **Protein rotation**: 4 main proteins keep variety while minimizing shopping

## Notes
- Don't subtract fiber from carb totals
- Spread protein evenly throughout the day
- Minimum 64oz water, target 100oz daily
- Each starchy veg serving ~15g carbs
