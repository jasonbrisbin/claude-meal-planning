## Meal Prep Tips
- **Sunday prep**: Hard boil 1 dozen eggs, cook white rice in bulk, wash and chop vegetables (broccoli, cauliflower, peppers, onions, cabbage)
- **Sunday evening**: Portion chicken breasts and pork loin chops for the week
- **Each dinner**: Cook ~1.5x portions to have leftovers ready for next day's lunch
- **Wednesday prep**: Make turkey meatballs in bulk (covers Tue dinner + Wed lunch)
- **Protein rotation**: Chicken (Mon/Thu/Sun dinners), Turkey (Tue/Sat dinners), Pork (Wed/Fri dinners)
