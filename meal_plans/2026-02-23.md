## Meal Prep Tips
- **Sunday prep**: Hard boil 1 dozen eggs, cook white rice in bulk, wash and slice vegetables (zucchini, peppers, mushrooms, onions)
- **Sunday evening**: Pre-portion pork tenderloin into daily servings
- **Wednesday prep**: Make beef and pinto bean chili (Thursday dinner + Friday lunch leftovers)
- **Protein rotation**: Chicken (Mon/Tue/Thu/Sat/Sun), Beef (Mon/Wed/Thu/Fri/Sat), Pork (Tue/Wed/Fri/Sun)

## Notes
- Don't subtract fiber from carb totals
- Spread protein evenly throughout the day
- Minimum 64oz water, target 100oz daily
- Each starchy veg serving ~15g carbs
- Fruit servings ~15g carbs
